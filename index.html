📚 Event Overview

SMCC-CCIS Week 2025 is the annual gathering of the College of Computing and Information Sciences. Join us for a week of hackathons, robotics, cybersecurity, and project showcases. Connect with peers, industry mentors, and innovators.

📅 Event Schedule

Day 1 – Opening Program & Keynote Talks
Day 2 – Coding Hackathon
Day 3 – Robotics & IoT Exhibition
Day 4 – Cybersecurity Awareness Session
Day 5 – Project Showcase & Awarding Ceremony

📍 Location & Map

📍 Atupan St., Brgy. 4, Nasipit, Agusan del Norte

📝 Event Registration

✓ All fields required. Student ID: 6–12 digits.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

• Who can join? All SMCC-CCIS students and faculty.
• Is registration free? Yes, it is free for all participants.
• Will certificates be given? Yes, e-certificates for attendees and winners.
